    • Group Project Profile

    In our group project, we developed a healthy diet management app called BellyBanter, which aims to help users develop healthy eating habits through a fun user interface and personalized health management plan. The app helps users better manage their daily diets by recording their meals in real time, analyzing calorie and nutrient intake, and providing health scores and personalized diet plans.
